⚖️ The Nutritional Hierarchy of Need

For scale weight manipulation, total daily calories dictate success or failure. If your macros are off, staying under your calorie ceiling will still guarantee weight loss.

However, for body composition and performance, hitting your protein target ensures you lose fat rather than muscle. Finally, while they can fluctuate, consistently neglecting your fat minimums will disrupt endocrine function (hormones), and neglecting carbs will destroy high-intensity athletic performance.

🍷 The Alcohol Variable (7 kcal/g)

Alcohol is highly calorific but is not considered a macronutrient because the body cannot store it for functional energy; it treats it as a toxin that must be oxidized immediately, halting fat burning in the process.

Dehydration Risk: Additionally, alcohol acts as a diuretic. Beverages above ~4% ABV actively dehydrate you, as the suppression of anti-diuretic hormone forces your body to expel more liquid than the drink contains.

Reference metrics: A standard pint of beer contains ~200-250 kcal. A large glass of wine (250ml) contains ~225 kcal. These must be subtracted from your carbohydrate or fat allowances.
